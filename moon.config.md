- Inbound leads going cold before a human rep responds

- B2B SaaS companies
- 50 to 5000 employees
- Uses Salesforce CRM
- Active inbound website traffic

# Not a fit for
- B2C or ecommerce brands
- Companies without Salesforce
- Early stage with no website traffic
