Who is this
actually for?

This is how Moon decides whether to offer a demo or give an honest exit. Be specific — vague criteria produce bad fit scores.

✓ BEST FIT FOR Press Enter to add
Company type, size, tech stack, revenue. Each item is a +2 to the fit score.
✗ NOT A FIT FOR Press Enter to add
Dealbreakers. Any match here triggers an honest exit — no demo offered. This builds trust.
THE ONE QUALIFYING QUESTION Optional
When Moon is unsure, it asks one question. What's your single most important qualifier?
